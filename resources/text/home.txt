The University of Waikato, Tauranga Campus has a cultural narrative built into its frame and woven into its fabric. The narrative is designed to bring cultural identity and heritage to the building and to celebrate our relationships across the Bay of Plenty and beyond. The artworks you are about to discover are the work of local, award winning artists who were inspired by the stories of Tauranga Moana and guided by kaumatua, along with university and architectural design advisers. It is an inclusive narrative that embraces the diversity of the cultural ethnicities that make up our campus community.
